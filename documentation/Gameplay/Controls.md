# SpaceRPG Controls

## Flight/Movement:
  Throttle Up: W
  
  Throttle Down: S
  
  Rotate Left: A
  
  Rotate Right: D
  
  Auto-Pilot: P
  
  Quantum Warp: Z
  
  Emergency Stop: X

## Combat
  Aim: Mouse
  
  Shoot:
  
    Turret: Mouse 1
    
    Missiles: Mouse 3
    
  Weapon Select:
  
    Turret: 1-4
    
    Missile: 5-8
    
    Misc: 9 (and 0)
    
    Doomsday: 0
